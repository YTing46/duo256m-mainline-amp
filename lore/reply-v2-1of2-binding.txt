Hi Junhui,

Two suggestions from testing this on SG2002 hardware (details in my
reply to patch 2/2):

1) The SG2000/SG2002 C906L subsystem is compatible with the CV1800B
   one - the driver works unmodified. Could v3 allow a
   per-SoC compatible with the cv1800b fallback, e.g.:

	compatible:
	  oneOf:
	    - const: sophgo,cv1800b-c906l
	    - items:
	        - enum:
	            - sophgo,sg2000-c906l
	            - sophgo,sg2002-c906l
	        - const: sophgo,cv1800b-c906l

2) The register block behind the sophgo,syscon phandle (SEC_SYS at
   0x020b0000) is only accessible while clk_efuse (CLK_EFUSE) is
   running. Mainline gates it via clk_disable_unused, after which any
   access wedges the SoC's interconnect - see my reply to 2/2 for the
   analysis and a devmem one-line reproducer. The syscon (or this
   node) should have a required clocks entry so the dependency is
   expressed in DT rather than relying on clk_ignore_unused.
